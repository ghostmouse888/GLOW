Privacy Policy for Glow
Last updated: April 2026

WHAT WE COLLECT
Glow collects the following information:
- Your name and age (entered during onboarding)
- Your mood check-ins and energy levels
- Journal entries and voice recordings
- Sleep logs and body check data
- Your device location (used only to find 
  local mental health resources nearby)
- Camera data (used only for facial training 
  exercises — nothing is recorded or stored)

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT
- We never sell your data
- We never share your data with advertisers
- Vent sessions are never saved anywhere
- We never store camera footage or images

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
- To personalize your Glow experience
- To generate AI insights from your mood 
  and journal history
- To find mental health resources near you
- To send you optional daily reminders

AI AND CLAUDE
Glow uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power 
chat, insights, and coaching features. 
Messages sent to Claude are processed 
securely and are not used to train AI models.
Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at 
anthropic.com/privacy.

DATA STORAGE
Your personal data is stored locally on 
your device using iOS secure storage. 
Anonymous usage data (daily mood pulse) 
is stored securely on Supabase servers.

YOUR RIGHTS
You can delete all your data at any time 
from the Me tab in the app. Uninstalling 
the app removes all locally stored data.
